About us

RAMESSES is a US-based fashion platform for men. Founded in 2022, RAMESSES is not just about clothing, it is about cultural movements and making bold statements through wardrobe, influenced by gothic, punk and Japanese cult styles, RAMESSES presents the spirit of the "goth ninjas" look. We bring the best avant garde styles that blends somberness and elegance. Our goal is to promote creativity, support designers from every corner of the world, push the boundaries of the industry, and most importantly to give back to the community and support its well being.
